This page is dedicated to helping the gluten-less people of the world find beer they can drink.

As someone with Celiac Disease who travels the United States a lot, I wanted to create a way to keep track of and easily recall Gluten-Free and Gluten-Reduced beers that I've had before. I'm hoping that this site can be a resource for other people as well.

Have a beer that you would like to share with us? Fill out the form on our Contribute page.
